Note: this is not a guide for going to the mountain or hiking, this is my
personal experaince of trying it(and I am writing it for my future

You may need similar things

I left home about 7am and I had these things in my bag: buttle of water
unberlla, bread and cheese, tempature sensor, mp3 player and pocket
tissues puls a good hat (mine is made in Germany). Plus two apple, a kinfe and small pice of blanket for sun protection.
I wake up about 6am, first drink about 1 liter water, after about 20
minutes then having breakfast.

Location

I went to closest mountain near to my place, one feautre I was looking
for was the mountain that is big and far enought from (noise of) city.
Another thing that was encourging me was finding an spot that is hard to
by others.

What was good about it

Well I haven't feel that much good since I backed home from my last
travel. I remember I was sitting in wide area between two mountains and
it was great to be far from all dependencies. I though about the movie
that tom hangs played, He was stuck in an island in the middle of ocean
no one to communicate expect a ball and no technology to help him to
servive expect his hands and his brain.
Finally he made a boat and left that Island in hope of finding people.
Personally I like that sence of the movie that he was sitting in his car
driving in the road after geting out of that island.
I saw a map and couple of buttle of water in his car (feeling good free).

Feeling good and tired and being home

I backed home about 7 pm, first I drink three glass of milk then had a
shower, It took about 7 minutes to wash my hair and body and about 2
minutes having cold shower. Cold shower makes me fresh and that is why I
am able to write about my hard day after that 2 minutes.
